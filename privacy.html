Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026
Last updated: March 30, 2026

willcall ("we," "us," or "our") operates the willcall app and website at willcall.app and willcall.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our services.

We take your privacy seriously. This policy is written in plain language so you know exactly what's happening with your data.

What willcall does

willcall is a social app for logging and cataloging your concert and live event attendance history. You can check in to shows, build a history of events you've attended, and connect with other concertgoers.

Information we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Your email address, display name, username, and profile details you choose to share (such as a bio or profile photo).
  • Concert and event data: Shows you log, check-ins, venues, artists, and any notes or photos you attach to events.
  • Connections: People you follow and who follow you within the app.

Information from third-party services

When you choose to connect a third-party service, we may collect information from that service to help build your concert history. You always initiate these connections — we never access third-party data without your permission.

  • Last.fm / ListenBrainz: If you connect your account, we access your listening history (scrobbles) to identify artists and concerts you may have attended. This data is accessed read-only through the service's public API.
  • Setlist.fm: We use Setlist.fm's API to retrieve concert setlist data, venue information, and event dates to match against your attendance history.
  • Ticketmaster: We use the Ticketmaster Discovery API to surface upcoming events and venue information.
  • Spotify (optional): If you upload a Spotify GDPR data export, we process the listening history contained in that file to identify potential concert matches. We do not connect to the Spotify API on your behalf.

Google Calendar data

If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, we access your calendar events solely to search for concerts, live shows, and events you may have attended. Specifically, we access:

  • Event titles and descriptions — to identify events that appear to be concerts, shows, or live performances.
  • Event dates and times — to log when you attended an event.
  • Event locations — to match events to known venues.

We do not access or store any calendar data unrelated to concert or event attendance. Calendar events that do not match concert or event patterns are discarded immediately and are never stored.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: How you interact with the app (pages viewed, features used, actions taken).
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and general location (city-level, derived from IP address).
  • Log data: IP address, access times, and referring URLs.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the willcall service, including building and displaying your concert attendance history.
  • Identify concerts and events from your connected services (including Google Calendar) and suggest matches for your history.
  • Display your profile and concert history to other willcall users, based on your privacy settings.
  • Send you transactional communications (account verification, password resets, feature updates).
  • Improve the app and develop new features.
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or security issues.

We do not use your data to serve ads, build advertising profiles, or sell to third parties.

Google API Services — Limited Use Disclosure

willcall's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only use Google Calendar data to provide and improve willcall's core user-facing features — identifying concerts and events for your attendance history.
  • We do not transfer Google Calendar data to third parties, except as necessary to provide the service (for example, matching an event to a known venue in our database).
  • We do not use Google Calendar data for advertising, retargeting, or interest-based profiling.
  • We do not use Google Calendar data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.
  • We do not allow humans to read your Google Calendar data, except with your express consent (such as for troubleshooting a support request you initiated), for security purposes, to comply with applicable law, or when the data is aggregated and anonymized for internal operations.

How we store and protect your data

  • Storage: Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure). Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access controls: Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only, on a need-to-know basis.
  • Credentials: OAuth tokens and authentication credentials are stored securely and are never exposed in client-side code or logs.
  • Third-party data: Google Calendar data used to identify concerts is processed and matched. Only the resulting concert/event records are stored in your willcall profile. Raw calendar data is not retained after processing.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • Your public profile: Your display name, username, profile photo, and concert history are visible to other willcall users based on your privacy settings.
  • Service providers: We use third-party services to operate the app (hosting, email delivery, analytics). These providers access only the data they need to perform their services and are bound by their own privacy obligations.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
  • Safety: We may share information if we believe in good faith that it is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of willcall, our users, or the public.

Your rights and choices

  • Access and export: You can view all of your concert history and profile data within the app at any time.
  • Correction: You can edit your profile information and concert history directly in the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual concert entries or request full account deletion. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data from our active systems. Some data may be retained in backups for a limited period before being permanently deleted.
  • Revoke third-party access: You can disconnect any linked service (including Google Calendar) at any time from your settings. When you disconnect Google Calendar, we stop accessing your calendar data. Previously identified concert records remain in your history unless you choose to delete them.
  • Opt out of communications: You can unsubscribe from non-essential emails at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hey@willcall.app.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Google Calendar data is processed at the time of connection and is not retained in raw form after concert matching is complete.

Children's privacy

willcall is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at hey@willcall.app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email before the changes take effect. Your continued use of willcall after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
